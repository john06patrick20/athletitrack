Find answers to common questions about using AthletiTrack based on your role.

How do I edit my profile or change my password?

Click on your name in the top-right corner of the navigation bar. From the dropdown menu, you can select "My Profile" to update your personal details or "Change Password" to update your login credentials.

How do I add new athletes to my team?

Navigate to the "My Athletes" (or "Athlete Roster") page. Click the "+ Add Athletes" button. In Step 1, select the team you want to add players to. In Step 2, you can fill out the details for multiple athletes at once. The form is expandable.

How do I schedule an event?

Go to the "Events" page and click "+ Schedule Events". If you are a coach, you will be taken directly to the scheduling form for your team. If you are an admin, you will first select the team you want to schedule for.

How do I enter stats for a game?

Navigate to the "Events" page and click on an event to view its details. In the "Participants" card header, click the "Enter Game Report" button. This will take you to a page where you can enter the final score and individual player statistics for that specific event.

How do I manage Teams, Sports, and Statistics?

Click on your user account dropdown in the top right and select "Settings". This page allows you to create new Teams, manage the list of available Sports, and define the specific performance statistics for each sport.
